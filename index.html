Got Questions?

Frequently Asked Questions

Here are answers to the most common queries we receive about scheduling, insurance coverage, and procedures. If you have other questions, please contact us!

Contact Our Team

Yes, we accept most major dental PPO insurance plans. Our dedicated team will verify your benefits, submit claims on your behalf, and help you maximize your coverage. We provide clear, itemized cost breakdowns before any treatment starts so there are never any financial surprises.

Your first visit will last about 60 minutes. It includes a friendly review of your dental and medical history, low-radiation digital dental X-rays, a comprehensive oral health examination (including gum disease and oral cancer screenings), a gentle clean, and a personal consultation to discuss findings and outline a customized dental care plan.

For most children and adults, we recommend a routine checkup and professional hygiene cleaning every six months. If you have a history of gum disease, frequent cavities, or compromised immune systems, we may recommend a customized hygiene schedule (every 3 to 4 months) to maintain your health.

If you experience a dental emergency—such as a severe toothache, a broken/chipped tooth, or a knocked-out permanent tooth—please call our clinic immediately at 9876543213. We keep emergency blocks open daily and will make every effort to schedule you for a same-day examination.
